How AutoLog Works

  • 1. Save your college WiFi username and password once.
  • 2. Connect to college WiFi (internet won't work yet).
  • 3. Open AutoLog from home screen.
  • 4. Tap the big AUTO LOG button.
  • 5. It automatically logs you in to the captive portal.
